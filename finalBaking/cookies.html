SALTED CARAMEL RICE KRISPIE TREATS

Salted Caramel Rice Krispie Treats are a decadent upgrade to the classic dessert, featuring the rich, buttery flavor of caramel paired with a hint of sea salt. This irresistible combination of sweet and salty elevates the familiar chewy texture of Rice Krispie treats into a gourmet snack or dessert.

×

Salted Caramel Rice Krispie Treats!

  • 6 cups Rice Krispies
  • ½ cup unsalted butter
  • ½ cup dark brown sugar
  • ¼ cup heavy whipping cream
  • 1 tbsp light corn syrup
  • 1 tsp kosher salt or flaked sea salt, plus more for sprinkling on top
  • 10 oz (plus 1 cup) mini marshmallows

Directions:

In a large, heat-proof bowl, toss together the Rice Krispies and 1 cup of mini marshmallows. Set aside. Spray an 8x8" baking pan with non-stick cooking spray and set aside. In a large, heavy pot, melt the butter over medium-high heat, stirring constantly. When it's melted, reduce the heat to medium and add the brown sugar, whipping cream, and corn syrup and cook for about 7 minutes, stirring frequently to keep the caramel from scorching. When the caramel is thick and syrupy, add in the salt and stir to combine, then remove from heat and add the 10 ounces of marshmallows. Stir until the marshmallows are dissolved, then quickly pour over the Rice Krispies and stir quickly to coat completely. Press into the prepared baking pan and sprinkle with sea salt. Allow to cool completely.

Tip:

Double the recipe to get thicker bars

Get more mini marshmallows and and them to the top once they are in the pan.

Mint Texas Sheet Cake

Mint Texas Sheet Cake is a refreshing twist on the classic Texas sheet cake, blending rich chocolate flavor with cool, invigorating mint. This decadent dessert features a moist chocolate cake base, infused with a hint of mint, and topped with a luscious chocolate-mint glaze or frosting. The combination of flavors creates a treat thats both indulgent and refreshing, perfect for celebrations or satisfying a craving for something unique

×

Mint Texas Sheet Cake

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup butter
  • 3 Tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup sour cream
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Frosting Ingredients

  • 6 Tablespoons milk
  • 3 Tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 ½ cup butter
  • ¾ cups powdered sugar

CAKE Directions

Add to saucepan: water, butter, and cocoa powder and bring mixture to a boil.

In a bowl: mix together the flour, sugar, baking soda, and salt.

In another bowl: mix together the sour cream, eggs and vanilla. Add sour cream/egg mixture to the flour mixture and mix until combined. Combine the batter.

Once the chocolate mixture in the saucepan is boiling, remove it from heat and pour it into the batter. Mix until combined and no lumps remain. Pour into a sheet pan. Pour mixture into the prepared pan and smooth with a spatula to make sure it is dispersed in an even layer. Bake. at 350°F for 15-20 minutes or until set (mine is usually done around 15 minutes).

FROSTING

In a saucepan: Add milk, cocoa and butter; bring mixture to a boil.Stir in: powdered sugar. I like to beat the mixture with my hand-held mixer to get rid of any lumps. Pour over cake. Pour the hot mixture right over the hot cake. Use a spatula to spread it evenly over the cake. Add your Andes Mints Allow frosting to set for about 10 minutes before eating.

JAM BARS

Jam bars are a delightful dessert that combines a buttery, crumbly crust with a sweet and tangy jam filling. These simple yet delicious treats are perfect for showcasing your favorite fruit jam, whether it's classic strawberry, raspberry, or a unique seasonal flavor. They are easy to make, versatile, and ideal for sharing.

×

Jam Bars

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups butter
  • 2 ¼ cup brown sugar
  • 3 cups flour
  • 3 cups oats
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 ½ bottles of raspberry jam (8oz)

Directions:

Mix all dry ingredients, and then press ⅔ mixture into a sheet pan. Bake for 12 minutes. Then let cool for 15 minutes. Then add your 1 ½ bottles of jam. Before crumbling on the other ⅓ of the mixture on top. Then bake again for 12-15 minutes. Bake at 350 degrees
